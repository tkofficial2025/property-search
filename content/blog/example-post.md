# Tokyo Expat Housing Guide - Getting Started

Welcome to our comprehensive guide on finding housing in Tokyo! This article will help you navigate the Tokyo real estate market.

## Understanding Tokyo's Housing Market

Tokyo's housing market is unique and can be challenging for newcomers. Here are some key points to keep in mind:

- **Location matters**: Proximity to train stations significantly affects prices
- **Size vs. Price**: Tokyo apartments are generally smaller but well-designed
- **Key Money**: Be prepared for initial costs including key money (礼金) and deposit (敷金)

## Popular Areas

### Shibuya
Shibuya is one of Tokyo's most vibrant districts, perfect for young professionals.

### Shinjuku
Shinjuku offers excellent transportation links and a mix of residential and commercial areas.

## Tips for Foreigners

1. **Language**: Many landlords prefer Japanese speakers, but this is changing
2. **Guarantor**: You may need a guarantor company
3. **Documentation**: Prepare all necessary documents in advance

Good luck with your housing search!
